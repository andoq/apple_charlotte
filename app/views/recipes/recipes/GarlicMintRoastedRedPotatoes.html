Garlic & Mint Roasted Red Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs. red new potatoes
  • 6 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 3/4 cup virgin olive oil
  • 1 large bunch fresh mint
  • 1 tablespoons kosher salt, or to taste
  • freshly ground pepper to taste

Method

  • Preheat oven to 350 F. Scrub the potatoes and prick each one several times with a fork. Place in a shallow roasting pan and roast for 2 hours. Cut each potato in half. Toss the potatoes with the galic, oil, mint, salt and pepper to taste in a large bowl. Let stand for 30 minutes before serving.

Notes

SERVINGS: 16
