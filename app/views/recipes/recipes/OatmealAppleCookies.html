Oatmeal Apple Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups uncooked oatmeal
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 apple, chopped

Method

  • Heat oven to 350F degrees.
  • Beat together margarine and sugars until creamy; add eggs and valilla and beat well.
  • Add flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt mixture and mix.
  • Stir in oats, raisins and apples and mix.
  • Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet and bake 10-12 minutes. Remove to wire rack.

Notes

SERVINGS: 48
