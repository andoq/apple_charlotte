Fudge Truffle Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups vanilla wafer crumbs
  • 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 24 ounces neufchatel cheese
  • 1 cup egg substitute
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Method

  • Preheat oven to 300F
  • Mix together crushed vanilla wafers, powdered sugar, cocoa and butter by hand. Press into a 9" springform pan.
  • In a double boiler, melt the chocolate chips, making sure that they are very smooth.
  • With an electric mixer beat cream cheese until fluffy. Gradually beat in condensed milk until smooth. Add melted chocolate, eggs, and vanilla. Beat on a low speed until the ingredients are thoroughly blended. Pour the filling into the prepared crust.
  • Bake at 300F for 55 minutes. The cake will seem underbaked in the center, but will continue to cook after you remove it from the oven.

Notes

Description:

"Top with chocolate dipped strawberries, chocolate truffles or

drizzling of melted chocolate and white chocolate."

SERVINGS: 14
