Lemon Cream Tart

Ingredients

  • 1 9" pie shell, baked and cooled
  • 1 recipe lemon curd, chilled thoroughly
  • 1 cup whipping cream, whipped
  • 1/2 pkg powdered gelatin
  • lemon zest

Method

  • Pour powdered gelatin over 2 tablespoons boiling water. Allow to sit a minute and mix. Cool slightly and add to whipping cream as it whips.
  • Mix chilled lemon curd with whipped cream and pile into pie crust. Top with lemon zest and chill in frig for 1 hour if possible. Description:
  • "A light and luscious ending to any meal."

Notes

SERVINGS: 8
