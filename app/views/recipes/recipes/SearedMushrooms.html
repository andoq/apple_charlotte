Seared Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • shallots, minced
  • salt and pepper
  • crimini mushrooms, or a mixture of mushrooms if desired
  • fresh thyme, minced
  • garlic, minced

Method

  • Saute shallots briefly. Add mushrooms, salt, pepper and thyme. Cook over high heat until mushrooms begin to turn gold and caramelize. At the end add garlic and cook briefly.

Notes

If using with wine reduction sauce, add to sauce right before serving, heating through.SOURCE: Madeleine Kamman
