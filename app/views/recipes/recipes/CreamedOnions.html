Creamed Onions

Ingredients

  • 5 large white onions, cut in half & sliced thin
  • 1 quart half and half
  • 4 tablespoons butter, "a glob"
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Method

  • Place onions in large pot of water and boil onions until transparent. Dump off water and add half and half to not quite cover and heat to almost boiling. Thicken with about one tablespoon of cornstarch added to a little milk and bring to boil for about a minute or two.
