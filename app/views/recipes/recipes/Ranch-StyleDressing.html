Ranch-Style Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup cider vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, mashed and minced
  • 3 tablespoons green onions, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley*, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • *Extra fresh herbs if desired, like..., thyme, rosemary, chives, etc.

Method

  • Shake all ingredients in a 1 quart jar with lid. Refrigerate.

Notes

Description:

"No...you won't be using the packaged version. Make it from scratch!

Great on baked potatoes, too."

SERVINGS: 8
