Greek Sparkling Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 piece lemon rind
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup lemon juice
  • cold sparkling
  • thin lemon slices
  • mint

Method

  • Dissolve sugar, water and lemon rind over medium heat. Add lemon juice. Cool in refrigerator. Before serving, add cold sparkling, lemon slices & mint.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8
