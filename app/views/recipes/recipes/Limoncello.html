Limoncello

Ingredients

  • 3 large bottles Everclear, or 100 proof vodka
  • 30 large lemons, grated on box grinder lightly...NO PITH
  • Infuse zest in bottles of Everclear for 40
  • days.
  • Make a simple syrup (2 quarts) and add to
  • bottles. Cork and seal with wax.
