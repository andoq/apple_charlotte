Tofu Filling for Chile Rellenos

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces silken tofu
  • 1 teaspoon Mexican oregano
  • 1/2 or to taste lemon, juiced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • drizzle of olive oil
  • salt & pepper to taste

Method

Notes
