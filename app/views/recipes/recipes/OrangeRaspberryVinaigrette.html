Orange Raspberry Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons fresh orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon raspberry vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • salt & freshly ground pepper
  • 2 teaspoons snipped chives

Method

  • Mix the orange juice and vinegar together in small bowl. Slowly add the oil, whisking constantly until smooth. Season to taste with salt & pepper and stir in chives. Makes about 1/2 cup.

Notes

NOTES:

Serving Ideas : Wonderful over baby greens, fresh asparagus or even fruit.NOTES : Make your own raspberry vinegar by taking fresh raspberries, placing in bottle that has been first placed in boiling water. Coverwith white vinegar. Let sit for at least 2 weeks and strain into new sterle bottle. Its so fresh and lovely!

SERVINGS: 6
