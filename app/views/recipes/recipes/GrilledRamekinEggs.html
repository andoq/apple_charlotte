Grilled Ramekin Eggs

Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs
  • olive oil spray
  • salt and pepper
  • chives, minced

Method

  • Spray ramekins with olive oil spray. Bread an egg into each ramekin (two eggs can be done at once in each ramekin). Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Place ramekin on pan for stability and place in grill. Close cover and grill for about 10-12 minutes. Top with minced chives and serve in ramekins.

Notes

Description:

"These are very pretty and fun around the outside grill on a sunny

Summer morning!"

SERVINGS: 12
