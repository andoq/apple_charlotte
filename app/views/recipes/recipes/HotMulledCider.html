Hot Mulled Cider

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts cider
  • 2 cinnamon stick
  • 5 whole cloves
  • 5 whole allspice berries
  • 2 whole oranges, sliced
  • 1 whole orange, peeling only

Method

  • Place altogether in a pot and heat until flavors are infused into the cider.

Notes

SERVINGS: 12
