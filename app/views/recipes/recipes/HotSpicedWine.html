Hot Spiced Wine

Ingredients

  • 1 quart dry red wine, fruity
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon whole cloves
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1/2 orange, sliced

Method

  • Combine all ingredients in large saucepan. Bring to boil over high heat, then simmer 15 minutes. Strain and serve hot.

Notes

SERVINGS: 10

SOURCE: Half Way House, Richmond, VA
