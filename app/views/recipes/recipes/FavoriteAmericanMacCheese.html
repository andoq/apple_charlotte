Favorite American Mac & Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. penne pasta, or pasta of choice, cooked
  • 3 cups white sauce, up to 4 cups if needed.
  • 2 1/2 cups cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted

Method

  • Mix hot white sauce with cheese. Mix in pasta. Pour into buttered cassarole dish.
  • Mix bread crumbs with 1/4 cup butter. Place on top of pasta and cheese mixture. Bake at 375F for about 20-30 minutes until top is golden.

Notes

Description:

"This is what we love from the time we are kids. Make the sauce, mix

with the noodles and eat away....or pop it all in a cassarole dish,

top with buttered breadcrumbs and bake. Wow!"

SERVINGS: 8
