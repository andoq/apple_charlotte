  • Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  • Place parsley, carrots, celery, garlic, and oregano in a food processor or blender and process until finely chopped. Grease a baking dish with olive oil. Layer remaining ingredients in the dish, sprinkling each layer with the parsley blend. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
