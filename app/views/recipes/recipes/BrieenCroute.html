Brie en Croute

Ingredients

  • 1 small round brie cheese
  • 1/2 package puff pastry, or one sheet
  • 2 tablespoons favorite chutney or jam

Method

  • Take brie out of wrapping and cut off rind on all sides.
  • Roll out puff pastry sheet to thin rectangle. Place brie round in the middle. Top with jam. Bring up corners and sides of puff pastry as though you are wrapping a package. Pinch at "waist" to secure. Place in freezer for a couple of hours (if you are going to do a couple to have extra, after freezing, wrap in plastic wrap and freeze for up to one month).
  • Take out of freezer and bake in preheated 425F oven for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. Serve immediately.

Notes

Description:

"This is an easy and delicious hors d'oeuvre to have around for any

time of year. Make it and freeze to have on hand during those busy

times of year."

SERVINGS: 8
