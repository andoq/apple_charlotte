Mango, Jicama and Cucumber Salad

Ingredients

  • 3 medium mangos, large cubes
  • 2 (1 1/2 pound) jicama, large cubes
  • 3 cucumbers, peeled, seeded, large cubes
  • 2 teaspoons ground chile
  • salt
  • 3 limes, juice

Method

  • Mix together, season to taste.

Notes

SERVINGS: 12

SOURCE: Rick Bayless
