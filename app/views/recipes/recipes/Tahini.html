Tahini

Ingredients

  • 1 cup tahini, with a little of its oil
  • 1 cup yogurt, or water
  • juice of 2 lemons
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon cumin

Method

  • Combine ingredients....taste for correct seasoning.

Notes

Description:

"This is a sauce served with Falafel, thinned for a salad

dressing...layered with tomatoes or cucumbers."

SERVINGS: 2

SOURCE: Adapted from Mark Bittman
