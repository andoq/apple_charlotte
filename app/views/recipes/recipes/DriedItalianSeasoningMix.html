Dried Italian Seasoning Mix

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons dried rosemary, crushed
  • 3 tablespoons dried basil, crushed
  • 3 tablespoons dried thyme, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon fresh ground pepper
  • Taste to balance taste.
