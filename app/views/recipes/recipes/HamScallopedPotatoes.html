Ham & Scalloped Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 8 red potatoes, cooked in skins
  • 2 cups ham, cubed
  • 1/2 bunch chives, chopped
  • 1 pint whipping cream
  • salt and pepper

Method

  • After cooking red potatoes, remove skin and cut into large dice. Mix with ham, chives and s/p and place in large au gratin dish. Pour whipping cream over and place in 400F oven for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and brown and some of cream has been asorbed.

Notes

Description:

"A fabulous and rich addition to any meal...wonderful as a brunch

item."

SERVINGS: 12
