Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 1/4 cups water, slightly warm
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons sugar

Method

  • Place 1/2 flour, yeast, water and oil in mixer. Mix for 5 minutes. Add rest of flour and mix with dough hook until mass forms, mixing another 2 minutes.

Notes

Yield:

"2 pizzas"SOURCE: Apple Charlotte
