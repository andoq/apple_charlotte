Norma's Huckleberry Dressing

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 5 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon grated onion
  • 1 cup huckleberries, fresh or frozen
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil

Method

  • Mix dry ingredients; add honey, lemon juice, vinegar and onion. Mix well. Slowly pour oil into mixture, beating constantly. Do not refrigerate!
  • This will keep indefinitely. Dressing is good over fruit salad, fresh vegetable and fruit salad. Makes about 2 cups.
