Joyce's Fabulous Apple Butter

Ingredients

  • 18 lbs. golden Delicious apple
  • 5 1/2 cups water
  • 3/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 5 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 9 cups brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups orange flavored liquor

Method

  • Throw all the apples into a large braising pan with water, lj. Cover and place in the oven, cooking until soft.
  • Put through food mill.
  • Add sugar, spices and bake in 350F oven for 1 1/2- 2 hours until thick.
  • Add liquor and put into jars and process in water bath for 10 minutes.

Notes

Yield:

"18 pints"
