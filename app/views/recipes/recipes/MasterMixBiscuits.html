Master Mix Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 cups master mix
  • 1/2 cup water

Method

Notes

Preheat oven to 400F. Add water and stir in with a fork. Don't overmix. Turn onto lightly floured board. Pull together. Pat into 1/2" thickness. Cut with biscuit cutter. Bake for 12-15 minutes.SOURCE: WSU Cooperative Extension
