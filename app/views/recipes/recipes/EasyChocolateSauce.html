Easy Chocolate Sauce

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces semi sweet chocolate
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Method

  • Place heavy whipping cream& milk in sauce pan and heat carefully. Cut up chocolate into medium chunks. When the cream and milk has come to a simmer, remove from heat. Add chocolate to the warm mixture. Allow the chocolate to sit in the milk mixture for about 5 minutes, then stir gently until smooth. Stir in the butter.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8
