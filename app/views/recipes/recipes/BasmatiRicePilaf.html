Basmati Rice Pilaf

Ingredients

  • 4 cups basmati rice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon onion, finely chopped
  • 8 cups chicken stock
  • 1 bay leaf

Method

  • Heat saute pan. Add olive oil and onions and cook until translucent. Add rice, making sure to coat each piece with oil.
  • Place onions and rice in rice cooker, add stock as instructed in rice cooker directions (it may vary slightly from 8 cups).
  • When rice is cooked, stir the rice, add salt to season to taste.

Notes

Description:

"Basmati rice is fragrant and delicious. This is a classic

preparation with the aid of a rice maker."

Yield:

"1/2 cup"

SERVINGS: 14
