Poached Pears

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups sauvagnon blanc, red wine can be used if desired
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 cinnamon stick
  • 4 anise stars
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 12 whole cloves
  • 6 pears, not too ripe

Method

  • Bring all to a boil and then simmer for about 20 minutes while preparing pears.
  • Peel pears, halve and take out core with melon baller. Lower the pears into the poaching liquid and poach until easily pierced with a skewer. Cool in some of the liquid. The liquid can be used again.

Notes

Description:

"These are terrific to have available for a salad or used in a

dessert. The amount of sugar and cooking time needs to be adjusted depending on the ripeness of the fruit."

SERVINGS: 12
