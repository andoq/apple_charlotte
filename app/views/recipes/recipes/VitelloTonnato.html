Vitello Tonnato

Ingredients

  • Bottom Round Veal
  • Roast to medium with m/p in
  • roasting pan
  • Cool in refrigerator, slice on slicer
  • Salsa Tonnato:
  • Simple mayonnaise
  • Canned tuna
  • Combine together
  • Add: capers, anchovies, s/p, lj
