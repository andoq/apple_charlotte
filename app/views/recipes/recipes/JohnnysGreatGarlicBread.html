Johnny's Great Garlic Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf French bread, split in half lengthwise
  • 1-2 cubes butter, softened
  • to taste cloves garlic, minced and smashed
  • (null) parsley, chopped
  • Worcestershire sauce, to taste
  • Kraft parmesan cheese, in green shakeable box

Method

  • Mix all ingredients together except parmesan Spread on both halves of bread. Sprinkle with lots of parmesan. Place under broiler until just crunchy,
