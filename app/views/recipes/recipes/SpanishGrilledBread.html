Spanish Grilled Bread

Ingredients

  • 1/2 loaf Italian bread, cut 1/3" thick
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 8 plum tomato, oven roasted

Method

  • Grill both sides of bread after brushing with o/o. Rub the hot bread with garlic clove on just one side. Then squeeze on tomatoes and rub across bread at the same time. Drizzle with o/o and season with s/p.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8
