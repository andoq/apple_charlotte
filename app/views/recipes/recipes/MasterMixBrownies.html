Master Mix Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup master mix
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Method

  • Preheat oven to 350F. Grease an 8" square pan. Mix dry ingredients. Add eggs and vanilla and mix. Pour into pan and bake for 25 minutes (do not overcook).
