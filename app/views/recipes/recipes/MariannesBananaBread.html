Marianne's Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 4 mashed bananas
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Method

  • Cream together sugar & butter until fluffy. Add bananas. Mix together flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, nutmeg and cinnamon. Mix well.

Notes

Pour into loaf pan. Bake 350 for 45 minutes-1 hour.SOURCE: Marianne Butterfield
