Shrimp Salad

Ingredients

  • Cooked bay shrimp, squeeze out juice
  • Select combinations of herbs &
  • seasonings from following: shallots,
  • onions, chives, garlic(mashed with salt),
  • tarragon, dill, curry, ginger
  • Mayonnaise to bind
  • Tiny amount (1/2 tsp.) breadcrumbs
  • if desired for consistency
  • Lemon juice, if desired
  • Good Combination: Shrimp, lemon, garlic
  • (mashed with salt), shallots, lots of dill
  • bound with dill flavored mayonaise .

Method

  • Blend together to taste...remembering will be served with Vinaigratte Dressing placed under artichoke.

Notes

SERVINGS: 1
