Blackened Fish Fillets

Ingredients

  • 8 snapper fillets
  • olive oil spray
  • Blackening Seasoning, recipe enclosed
  • lemon wedges

Method

  • Heat a cast iron or outside grill to high until smoking hot. Spray each fillet liberally with olive oil spray. Sprinkle 1 side of the filet with the seasoning and place in skillet or on grill (grill should be sprayed first). Cook about 2 minutes until charred. Turn and cook about 2 more minutes on other side.
  • Serve with polenta topped lightly with Southern Comfort Barbeque Sauce. Place fish on top. Serve with lemon wedges.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8

SOURCE: Judy Adams-Smith
