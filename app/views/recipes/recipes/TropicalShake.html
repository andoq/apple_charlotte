Tropical Shake

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana, 1"slices
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 medium mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups pineapple-orange juice, chilled
  • 1 cup nonfat vanilla yogurt

Method

  • Toss banana with lemon juice, drain reserving lemon juice. Place banana slices on a baking sheet and freeze 1 hour and 30 minutes.
  • Process frozen banana slices, reserved lemon juice, mango, 1 1/2 cups pineapple-orange juice and yogurt in a blender until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides. Pour into chilled glasses and serve immediately.

Notes

Yield:

"5 cups"

SERVINGS: 5
