Tropical Fruit Salsa

Ingredients

Method

  • 1 mango*
  • 1 papayas, peeled, seeded, dice*
  • 1 fresh pineapple, peeled, smal dice*
  • 3 Roma tomatoes, peeled, seeded, dice
  • 1 small red or Spanish onion, small dice
  • 2 jalapenos, seeded, minced
  • lime juice, fresh squeezed
  • 3 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons mint, chopped
  • Mix all fruit ingredients. Taste for correct seasoning. Enjoy on fish, pork, chicken or with tortilla chips.

Notes

*Any one or all three of these fruits can be used depending on what is available. SOURCE: Apple Charlotte
