Creamy Horseradish

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1 large horseradish, or bottled pickled horseradish
  • vinegar, to taste
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Method

  • Grate horseradish and place in sour cream. Taste for proper acid, salt and pepper to taste. This dish is all to taste....hot or hotter?

Notes

SERVINGS: 12
