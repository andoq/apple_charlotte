Peppery Greens with Peaches & Apricots

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches watercress, leaves removed
  • 2 bunches arugula, stems removed
  • 2 heads radicchio, broken
  • 2 cups whole dry dates, chopped
  • seasonal fruits: melon, peaches, mango,
  • apricots
  • 1 large red onion
  • 3 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Method

  • Vinaigrette: Whisk vinegar, lime juice, olive oil and salt and pepper. Taste for seasoning. Lightly toss some of the vinaigrette with greens. Plate with fruit and surround salad with vinaigrette.

Notes

SERVINGS: 10
