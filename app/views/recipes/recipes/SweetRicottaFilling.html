Sweet Ricotta Filling

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whipping cream
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 cup pistachios, toasted and chopped
  • 2 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 ounce raisins
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 recipe Homemade Ricotta Cheese

Method

  • Whip whipping cream and 1 T sugar to soft peaks.
  • Mix together pistachios, chocolate, raisings and sugar. Mix into ricotta cheese.
  • Fold whipping cream into ricotta mixture.
  • Ready for use for cake or cannoli filling.
