Tomato Couscous Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups couscous
  • salt
  • 3 cups boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups diced tomato, 1/4" dice
  • 1 cup parsley, chopped
  • 4 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 4 dashes Tabasco sauce
  • 1 cup Tomato Salad Dressing

Method

  • Mix couscous with salt in a large bowl. Add boiling water and oil. Cover and allow to stand for about 10 minutes.
  • Fluff the couscous with a fork and add tomato, parsley, and 2 tablespoons of the lemon juice, Tabasco and dressing. Stir gently with fork. Taste and correct seasonings.
  • Can be made up to 3 hours in advance of serving.

Notes

SERVINGS: 10
