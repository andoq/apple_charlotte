Char's Fresh Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup semolina flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 5 large egg yolks

Method

  • Place all dry ingredients in food processor. Mix briefly.
  • Lightly mix together in a small bowl the rest of the ingredients and add to the dry. Mix with food processor until mixed. About 45 seconds.
  • Pour out of processor; knead slightly and place bowl over for at least 20 minutes, up to 30 minutes.
  • Roll out into sheets with a pasta machine and made into desired shapes.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8
