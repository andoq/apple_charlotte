Christmas Tree Decorating Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. Italian Sausage, Out of casings
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 4 8 oz cans tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • pepper
  • Italian seasoning to taste
  • 3/4 cup red wine
  • 8 ounces light cream cheese
  • 2 cups plain lowfat yogurt
  • 12 scallion, minced
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 16 ounces lasagna noodles, broken into 1" pc.

Method

  • Saute sausage in large skillet until pink is gone and drain fat. Add garlic and saute. Add the next 5 ingredients and bring to boil. Simmer sauce for 20-30 minutes. Cook noodles. Meanwhile mix cream cheese, yogurt and scallions.
  • Place half of noodles in bottom of 9x12 casserole and top with half of sauce, half of cream cheese mixture and half of cheeses. Repeat this process with the other half of the ingredients beginning with lasagna noodles. Bake 30-40 minutes in 325 F. degree oven.

Notes

NOTES:

Serving Ideas : Needs only a huge green salad and garlic bread.NOTES : This is a simple and always terrificly colorful Christmas season supper for a group of friends or family. My mom made this once for a big party she had when I visited her in Washington D.C. many years ago. I have making it ever since and everyone always loves it...I like it better than your typical lasagna and its lots easier. Make an outrageous dessert like Tirimisu or Italian Christmas Cake and it will be the hit of the season!!

SERVINGS: 10
