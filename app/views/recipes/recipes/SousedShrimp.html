Soused Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. frozen shrimp, deveined, peeled
  • 1/2 bottle white wine
  • bouquet garni: parsley stems
  • bay leaves
  • thyme
  • pepper corns
  • 1 lemon, quartered
  • 1 orange, quartered
  • 1 lime, quartered
  • pickling spice
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 5 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1 sweet red peppers, cut small pieces
  • 1 sweet yellow pepper, cut small pieces

Method

  • 1. Pour the wine, bouquet garni, citrus, pickling spices and about 2-3 cups water into a pan for pouching. Bring to a boil and simmer for about 20 minutes.
  • 2. Add frozen shrimp to pouching liquid and cook until done, about 5 minutes or less.
  • Refrigerate to chill.
  • 3. Mix all the herbs and garlic. Add mustard and lemon juice and slowly whisk in olive oil. Add peppers. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  • 4. Add the herb/olive oil mixture to shrimp, mix gently. Add hot red pepper flakes if desired.

Notes

SERVINGS: 12
