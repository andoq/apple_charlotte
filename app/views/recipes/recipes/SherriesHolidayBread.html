Sherrie's Holiday Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon extract, These four items creamed
  • 4 large eggs, added one at a time
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 teaspoon soda
  • 1 cup jam, or yams, or cranberry sauce
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Method

  • 350F for 50 minutes until done in 2 buttered loaf pans.
