Tapenade

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. black olives, Italian or Kalamata
  • 4 anchovy fillets, rinsed
  • 3 tablespoons capers, drained
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • black pepper

Method

  • Place everything in a food processor except the olive oil. Pulse and pour in the olive oil to form a coarse or finely chopped mixture, as you prefer.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8
