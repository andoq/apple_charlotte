Indonesian Spicy Peanut Sauce

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • several generous shakes of Tabasco
  • sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (or to taste)

Method

  • Puree everything in processor and adjust seasonings for taste.

Notes

NOTES:

NOTES : Use this sauce when grilling meat, chicken or in stir-frys.

SERVINGS: 1

SOURCE: Hot & Spicy
