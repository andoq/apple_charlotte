Joanne's Homemade Toffee

Ingredients

  • 4 cups sugar
  • 1 lb. butter
  • 1 lb. margarine
  • 4 tablespoons white corn syrup
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 5 8 ounce Hershey Bars
  • 16 ounces almonds, ground in processor

Method

  • Cook the above ingredients over medium heat, stirring constantly, until sugar dissolves and candy thermometer reads 285 degrees. Take off heat, stirring constantly until temperature reads 287 degrees+. Pour immediately into 2 buttered cookie trays (10 x 15" , not totally filled). You may be tempted to lick this mixture at this very moment....BUT it is very hot and your tongue will remain with the candy on the spoon...so forget it. Cool this mixture.
  • Melt the Hershey bars in the microwave. Spread half the chocolate on the toffee with a rubber....ah, er...spatula. Sprinkle on ground almonds. Wait until chocolate and candy is hard ( a couple of hours). Take candy out of cookie tray and turn over on waxed paper. Spread with remaining chocolate and wait until hard. Break into pieces (no testing allowed as it will never get to anyone as a gift). Wow...wow...wowy.
