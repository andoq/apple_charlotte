Italian Christmas Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 Cake Rum Cake (recipe follows)
  • 1 lb. rocotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons whipping cream
  • 6 tablespoons dark rum
  • 1/4 cup candied fruit, chopped
  • 1/4 cup semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup almonds or walnuts, chopped

Method

  • Slice ends and crust off csake and cut horizontally into four lyers. Beat the ricotta with sugar, cream and rum until smooth. Fold in rest of ingredients and spread between layers. Top with Basic Chocolate Sauce (recipe follows) and garnish with candied fruit.

Notes
