Bibim Bap....Rice Medley

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups round steak, julienned
  • 2 teaspoon each soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar
  • pepper to taste
  • 4 teaspoons garic, minced
  • 4 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 16 shitake mushrooms, rehydrated, cut in thin strips
  • 2 cups each carrots, cucumber, shredded
  • 4 cups Spinach Salad
  • 2 cups Spicy Daikon Salad
  • 2 cups Bean Sprout Salad
  • 8 eggs, scrambled
  • 4 cups lettuce, shredded
  • 8 tablespoons Bibam Bap Sauce

Method

  • Make Spinach Salad, Spicy Daikon Salad and Bean Sprout Salad. Set aside or refrigerate.
  • Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, pepper and garlic with the beef. Cook in hot wok just until color changes. Remove from pan and keep warm.
  • Heat the 4 teaspoons of sesame oil in the wok. Add mushrooms, carrots and cucumber. Remove from pan. Add eggs and scramble.
  • Fill rice bowls with rice about 1/2 way. Add to each: meat, mushrooms/carrot/cucumber mixture, salads, eggs, lettuce and sauce. Finish with a sprinkle of sesame oil.

Notes

"Korean Cuisine"

SERVINGS: 8
