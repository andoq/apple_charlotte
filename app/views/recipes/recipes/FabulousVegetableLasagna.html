Fabulous Vegetable Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds mushrooms, seared and seasoned
  • 2 large onions, sliced & grilled
  • 2 large eggplants, salted, drained, roasted
  • 3 zucchini, sliced long & grilled
  • 2 bulbs fennel, sliced & grilled
  • 1 large package fresh basil, about 2 cups packed leaves
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 recipe Marinera Sauce
  • 1 recipe Char's pasta

Method

  • Cook all vegetables as directed.
  • Layer freshly rolled pasta in 9x13" pan, alternating ingredients with cheese & basil on each layer. End with a layer of pasta, sauce and cheeses.
  • Bake at 375F for 40-45 minutes until hot, bubbly and puffy.

Notes

There is no need to cook the pasta first before layering it in the pan.SOURCE: Apple Charlotte
