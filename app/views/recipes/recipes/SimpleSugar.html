Simple Sugar

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water

Method

  • 1. Mix the sugar and water in a small pot. Bring to a boil. Boil for 1 minute.
  • 2. Cool the syrup, cover, and store it indefinitely in the refrigerator.

Notes

NOTES:

NOTES : Yield on this recipe is 3 1/2 cups.

SERVINGS: 1
