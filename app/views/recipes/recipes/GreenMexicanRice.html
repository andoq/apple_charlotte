Green Mexican Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 anaheim chile
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons mint, chopped
  • splash of lime is delicious

Method

  • In the processor finely chop chilies. In a large skillet, melt butter and saute the chiles briefly. Stir in the cilantro and oniona nd toss for 15 seconds more. Add the mint (or oregano) and then rice.

Notes

Source:

"The Feast of Santa Fe"

SERVINGS: 4
