Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients

  • 1 Pork Tenderloin
  • olive oil
  • fresh herbs

Method

  • All in One Pan: Above with zucchini,
  • yellow zucchini, apple, s/p, honey before
  • placing in oven.
  • Cover tenderloin with o/o, herbs and s/p. Let sit at room temp for about 1/2 hr.
  • Heat saute pan until hot and sear tenderloin all around.

Notes

Pop in 400 F oven and cook to instant read thermometer reads 150F. Tent tenderloin with aluminum foil and allow to rest for about 10 minutes before carving.SOURCE: Char
