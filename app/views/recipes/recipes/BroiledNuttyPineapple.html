Broiled Nutty Pineapple

Ingredients

  • 1 whole ripe pineapple
  • 1 1/2 cups coarsely chopped nuts
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 6 tablespoons butter, melted, cooled

Method

  • Heat the broiler. Cut off the top and bottom of the pineapple. Set on its bottom and peel by slicing down the pineapple, all around.
  • Cut into slices and cut out center with cookie cutter.
  • Mix together the rest of the ingredients. Place pineapple slices on parchment paper on sheet pan and divide the topping between the pineapple.
  • Place under the broiler and caramelize about 2-3 minutes. Delicious served warm and with whipped cream if desired.

Notes

SERVINGS: 6
