Spinach & Turkey Pita Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground turkey
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 package frozen spinach, thawed, chopped
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • 1 cup olives, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • 1 cup plain low-fat yogurt
  • 1/2 cup fat-free mayonnaise
  • 6 pita breads, whole wheat fat free, warmed
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • lettuce

Method

  • In large skillet, brown ground turkey, stirring until cooked through. If any fat remains, drain. Meanwhile, place spinach in strainer; press with back of spoon to remove as much moisture as possible and roll in paper towel to further remove moisture. Add spinach, green onions, olves and 1/2 teaspoon pepper to cooked turkey. Cook about 2 minutes. Stir in tomato.
  • In small bowl, combine yogurt, mayo and remaining pepper. Split open pita breads. Line inside with lettuce. Stir cheese into turkey mixture and divide among pita pockets. Serve withyogurt sauce.

Notes

SERVINGS: 6
