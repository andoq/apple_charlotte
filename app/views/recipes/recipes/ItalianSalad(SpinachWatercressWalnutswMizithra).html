Italian Salad (Spinach, Watercress & Walnuts w/Mizithra)

Ingredients

  • 3 bunches fresh spinach
  • 2 bunches fresh watercress or arugula
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 radicchio
  • toasted walnuts
  • 1 hunk of mizithra cheese
  • Vinaigrette
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 garlic clove
  • apple cider vinegar
  • balsamic vinegar

Method

Notes

SERVINGS: 12
