Almond Soup (Zupa Migdalowa)

Ingredients

  • 5 cups milk
  • 1/2 lb. blanched almonds, ground twice
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup raisins

Method

  • Heat milk just to simmering in a large saucepan. Add all the ingredients; stir until well mixed. Cook over low heat 3-5 minutes. Serve hot as is traditional for Christmas. Great chilled as leftovers for Christmas morning (if you can get to it before John).
