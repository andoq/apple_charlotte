Honey Cornbread for a Crowd

Ingredients

  • 9 cups flour
  • 4 cups sugar
  • 3 cups corn meal
  • 6 tablespoons baking powder
  • 3 teaspoons salt
  • 7 1/2 cups milk
  • 12 large eggs
  • 2 cups oil
  • 18 tablespoons butter

Method

  • honey to cover top when cooked

Notes

Description:

"This makes 3 oblong pans (9 1/2 x 11") of cornbread"

SERVINGS: 48
