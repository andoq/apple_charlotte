Triple Chocolate "Mess"

Ingredients

  • 1 box devil's food cake mix
  • 1 small box chocolate instant pudding and pie filling
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 1 small bag chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup vegetabe oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup water

Method

  • Spray crockpot with nonstick cooking spray. Mix all ingredients until smooth and pour into crockpot. Cook on low for 6-8 hours.
  • Serve with ice cream in bowls.
