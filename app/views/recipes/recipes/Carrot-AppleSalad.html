Carrot-Apple Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 6 ounce can frozen orange juice concentrate
  • 1/2 6 ounce can water
  • 2 lbs. carrots, scraped and shredded
  • 3 tart apple, peeled, cored, shredded
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup golden raisins
  • 1 cup cashews, optional, coarsely chopped

Method

  • Place the juice and water in a large bowl and mix. Add in and combine well the remaining ingredients excpet the cashews, which should be tossed in at serving time.

Notes

Description:

"A wonderful and very healthy salad that kids love too!"

SERVINGS: 10
