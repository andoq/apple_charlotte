Cheese & Roasted Vegetable Pate

Ingredients

  • 1 8 oz cream cheese, room temp
  • 1 4 oz butter, room temp
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 3 scallions, minced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, roasted, julienned
  • 1/2 lb. spinach, sauteed in o/o and shallots; cooled

Method

  • Mix cream cheese, butter, parm, scallions and garlic. Press 1/3 mixture in small pate or loaf pan. Cover with peppers. Press on 1/3 cheese mixture on top. Add
  • spinach (squeezed of juice) and top with remaining third of cream cheese mixture.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8
