Chicken Tostadas

Ingredients

  • 8 store-bought corn tortillas
  • 2 cooked chicken breasts or thighs, shredded
  • beans of your choice: can use
  • Mexican beans (included in this pkt),
  • purchased beans, chili beans
  • lettuce, cut up
  • tomatoes
  • salsa, homemade (in this pkt) or
  • purchased
  • green onions, sliced
  • avacados or quacamole
  • shredded cheese of your choice

Method

  • Toast tortillas in oven at 350F for about 7 minutes and then turn over and toast the other side until crisp (make a bunch more and break them up and make nachos too).
  • Meanwhile, heat the beans, cut up the lettuce, dice the tomatoes, slice the green onions and avacados. Shred some cheese....about one cup.
  • Assembly: Place one or two tortillas on each individual plate. Top with beans, a bit of chicken (if desired), a small amount of cheese. You can place in microwave for a minute at this point or in the oven to melt the cheese. Take out of oven, top with lettuce, tomatoes, avacados, and salsa. Garnish with sliced onions.......yum. Cool and easy!

Notes

NOTES:

NOTES : This is a wonderful...."kids can start" recipe. If you have time make the beans in advance....if not, purchased beans are great too. This is the real Mexican style.

SERVINGS: 4
