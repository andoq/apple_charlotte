Ricotta-Parm Savory Filling

Ingredients

  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup Parmesan Reggiano, finely grated
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup parsley, chopped finely
  • 10 scrapes fresh nutmeg

Method

Notes

Mix together all the ingredients. Use as a pasta filling; i.e., lasagna, large shells, ravioli, etc.SOURCE: Apple Charlotte
