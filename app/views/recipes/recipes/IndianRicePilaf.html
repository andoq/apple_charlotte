Indian Rice Pilaf

Ingredients

  • 4 cups basmati rice
  • 10 whole cardamom pods
  • salt

Method

  • Put the rice in a bowl and cover generously with water. Leave to soak for 30 minutes. Drain thoroughly.
  • In heavy pan, combine the rice, 6 1/3 cups water and the cardamom pods. Bring to a boil. Cover very tightly, and turn heat very low. Cook for about 25 minutes. Leave pot covered and undisturbed for 10 minutes or move. Remove cardamom and salt to taste.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8
