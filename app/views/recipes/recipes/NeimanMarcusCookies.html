Neiman Marcus Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups butter
  • 4 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons soda
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 5 cups blended oatmeal, spin in Cuisinart to fine powder
  • 24 ounces chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 8 oz. Hershy Bar (grated)
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 3 cups chopped nuts

Method

  • Cream butter and both sugars. Add eggs, vanilla, mix together with flour, oatmeal, salt, baking powder and soda. Add rest of ingredients...
  • Roll into balls and place 2" apart on sheet. Bake 10 minutes @ 375 degrees.

Notes

SERVINGS: 112

SOURCE: Famous Neiman Marcus
