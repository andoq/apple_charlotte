Grilled Salmon

Ingredients

  • 7 cups water
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 1/2 pounds salmon fillet, skined and cut into portion sizes
  • salt & pepper to taste

Method

  • Mix water, salt and maple syrup to dissolve. Place salmon fillet into the mixture and brine for at least 30 minutes up to 6 hours.
  • Remove from brine and dry off with paper towels. Heat grill to smoking hot (inside grill pan or outside grill). Place salmon on grill pan and cook approximately 3 minutes per side (a little more if thick, a little less if thin). Remove to hot platter or warmed plates.
