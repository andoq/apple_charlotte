Apple Charlotte Focaccia

Ingredients

  • Biga:
  • 2 cups flour, bread or all purpose
  • 1 1/3 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • Dough:
  • Biga from above
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 5 cups flour
  • 1 tablespoon sea salt
  • Topping:
  • olive oil
  • 3 sprigs rosemary, leaves removed, minced
  • 6 leaves sage, minced
  • 2 teaspoons anise seed
  • chile flakes
  • kosher salt

Method

  • Make biga. Allow to sit, uncovered, for at least 1-2 hours; overnight is best.
  • Place biga in mixing bowl. Add water, wine and olive oil and half of remaining flour. Mix with a paddle attachment for about 5 minutes. Change to dough hook. Slowly add (1/2 cup at a time) the rest of the flour until mixed through. The dough should be slightly wetter than a bread dough.
  • Cover with plastic. Allow to rise for about 2 hours until double. Punch down. Allow to rise again for about 1 hour. Punch down and lay in 2 pizza pans that have been oiled and sprinkle with corn meal. Cover and allow to rise another 45-60 minutes. Meanwhile heat oven to 450F. When dough has risen, dimple with fingers and pour olive oil over. Sprinkle with herbs and seasonings. Place in oven and bake for about 20-30 minutes. If begins to cook too quickly, lower oven temperature to 425F.
