Italian Chopped Salad

Ingredients

  • red wine vinegar vinaigrette, :red wine vinegar, oil, s/p, garlic, shallot
  • 2 heads finely chopped romaine
  • 2 heads finely chopped radicchio
  • 1 bunch finely chopped parsley
  • 2 cans white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 15 oil packed sun dried tomatoes, drained, plotted and chopped
  • 1 cup chopped olives

Method

  • Place a bit of your vinaigrette in the bottom of a large bowl and add chopped ingredients. Mix gently adding more of prepared vinaigrette as needed. Taste to correct seasoning.

Notes

SERVINGS: 12
