Salame di Cioccolata

Ingredients

  • 1 jelly roll cake (can be found in any
  • general cookbook)
  • espresso coffee made
  • milk
  • Nutella

Method

  • Take cake out of pan. Spread with coffee to soak; sprinkle milk over that. Cover with Nutella and roll gently with a towel.
  • Place in refrigerator to cool until service
  • .
