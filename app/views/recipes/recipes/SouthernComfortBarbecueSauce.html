Southern Comfort Barbecue Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 lb. canadian bacon, lean
  • olive oil spray
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, minced
  • 3 tablespoons Southern Comfort
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 14 ounces ketchup

Method

  • Cook bacon until almost done. Add bell peppers, onion and saute until soft. Add Southern Comfort and reduce slightly. Add remaining ingredients and simmer for about 30 minutes on low.

Notes

Description:

"Fabulous over any grilled item!"

SERVINGS: 6
