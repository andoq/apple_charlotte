Balsamic Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic (1 if large), minced
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • 2 leaves basil, minced
  • 1/2 cup Balsamic vinegar
  • 1 1/2 cups olive oil
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • (null) a little honey, if desired

Method

  • Incorporate garlic, basil and Balsamic. Whisk in olive oil, incorporating. Salt & pepper to taste.

Notes

SERVINGS: 1
