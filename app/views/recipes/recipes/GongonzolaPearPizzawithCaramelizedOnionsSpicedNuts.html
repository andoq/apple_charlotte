Gongonzola Pear Pizza with Caramelized Onions & Spiced Nuts

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe pizza dough
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 onions, halved and sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 ounces gorgonzola cheese, or blue cheese of your choice
  • 3 pears, cored and sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups spiced nuts, chopped

Method

  • Make dough as directed. Allow to rise.
  • While dough is rising. Place 2 tablespoons of olive oil into hot saute pan. Add onions, salt and pepper. Cook about 20-30 minutes until onions are slightly brown and caramelized.
  • Cool.
  • Roll into two pizza size shapes and place in pizza pan or on sheet pan.
  • Mix garlic with olive oil. Brush on pizzas. Divide onions between pizzas and place on top of garlic/oil mixture. Divide the gorgonzola in half and sprinkle all but about 2 tablespoons per pizza (saving for the top) over the onions. Place the sliced pears in a circular pattern around the pizzas. Divide the nuts into two portions and top the pears with the nuts. Top with the last of the gongonzola.

Notes

Place on preheated pizza stone and 500F oven. Cook 10-15 minutes. SOURCE: Apple Charlotte
