Amazin' Raisin Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 cups unsifted flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup Best Foods mayo
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 3 cups apples, chopped, peeled
  • 1 cup seedless raisins
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, coarsely chopped

Method

  • Preheat oven to 350F. Grease and flour two 9" round cake pans.
  • Mix all ingredients except apples, raisins, walnuts. Beat at low speed for 2 minutes. Scrap bowl occasionally. Stir in apples, raisins, nuts. Pour pans and bake for about 45 minutes or until tester comes out clean.

Notes

Cool in pans for 10 minutes before removing to racks. Cool. Fill and frost with whipped cream or a light butter cream or cream cheese frosting. SOURCE: Adapted from Best Foods Ad 11/80
