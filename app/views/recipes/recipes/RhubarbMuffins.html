Rhubarb Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 1/2- 2 cups rhubarb, diced
  • 2 1/2 cups unsifted flour
  • 1/2 cups walnuts, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Method

  • Combine sugar, oil, egg and vanilla in bowl. Stir in buttermilk.
  • Combine dry ingredients and add to other mixture. Stir in rhubarb & nuts. Place in greased or papered muffin tins.
  • Topping: 1/3 cup sugar, 1 tablespoon butter, 1 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Notes

Sprinkle over muffins in pan. Press in. Bake at 400F for 20 minutes.SOURCE: Sue Oliveri
