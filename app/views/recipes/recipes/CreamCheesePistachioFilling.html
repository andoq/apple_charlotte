Cream Cheese & Pistachio Filling

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces cream cheese
  • 3 tablespoons chives, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon Italian parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup pistachio nuts

Method

  • Cream together and press tightly into bread cavity. Allow to set for about 1/2 hour before slicing.

Notes

Description:

"Used in a thin French loaf that has been sliced crosswise and scooped

out leaving only approximately 1/4" bread all around."SOURCE: James Beard
