Pasta Forne

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. cooked pasta, La Molisana (little "O's"); nelini
  • marinara sauce
  • ground meat, usually hamburger
  • cubed mortadella
  • cubed salami
  • cubed pancetta
  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • fresh bread crumbs
  • If desired: Line baking plate with thinly
  • sliced eggplant, bottom sides and then top
  • when filled with mixture above.

Method

  • Or....just place all in pan and cook through about 45 minutes.
