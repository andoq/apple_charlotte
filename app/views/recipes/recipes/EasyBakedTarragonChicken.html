Easy Baked Tarragon Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 cup creme fraiche
  • 1/2 cup dry sherry
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 4 tablespoons dried (or fresh 8 tablespoons)
  • tarragon, crumbled
  • freshly ground black pepper/salt
  • 8 boneless skinless chicken breast halves

Method

  • Preheat over to 350F
  • In a 13x9" baking dish stir together all ingredients except chicken. Add chicken to mixture, turning it to coat well, and arrange in one layer. Bake chicken in middle of oven 30 minutes, or until cooked through.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8
