Mexican Hot Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onions, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 large can fire grilled tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 jalapeno chile pepper, sliced
  • 2 teaspoons ground chipolte or chipolte-flavored
  • Tabasco
  • 2 teaspoons Mexican oregano
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1/2-1 lime juice, fresh

Method

Notes

Place all ingredients in food processor and add cilantro and lime to taste. Puree to desired consistency. Taste for salt and spiciness. Add more chiles if desired.SOURCE: Apple Charlotte
