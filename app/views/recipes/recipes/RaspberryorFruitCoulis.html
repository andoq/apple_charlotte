Raspberry or Fruit Coulis

Ingredients

  • 2 pints raspberries
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon brandy, rum or framboise

Method

  • Place fruit in blender or food processor. Blend until small pieces. Add sugar, slowly, tasting until desired sweetness is meant. Add liquor if desired. Ready for service.

Notes

Description:

"Change the fruit and cut up and make in the same fashion, tasting to

completion."

SERVINGS: 16

SOURCE: Apple Charlotte
