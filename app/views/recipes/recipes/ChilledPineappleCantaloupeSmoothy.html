Chilled Pineapple Cantaloupe Smoothy

Ingredients

  • 3 whole cantaloupe
  • 4 cups soy milk, low-fat
  • 3 cups pineapple juice, or mango juice
  • 12 sprigs mint

Method

  • Cut the cantaloups in half, scoop out seeds, and cut flesh into chunks. Put in a food processor or blender with the soy milk and pineapple (or mango) juice. Process until the mixture is smooth. Transfer to a large bowl and chill in freezer for a least 2 hours, stirring about every hour.
  • Serve the frothy beverage in pretty glasses with a sprig of mint.

Notes

Description:

"Delicious and healthy for snack."

Source:

"Adopted from The Whole Soy Cookbook"

Yield:

"1 cup"

SERVINGS: 12
