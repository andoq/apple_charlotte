Salad with the Works

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 head red leaf lettuce
  • 1 head curly leaf lettuce
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce
  • vegetables of choice....seasonal is best, even berries added are delicious
  • 1/2 lb. bacon, cooked and broken
  • 1/2 lb. Gorgonzola cheese
  • 6 eggs, hard boiled, chopped
  • Vinaigrette:
  • 1 large shallot
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 red wine vinegar

Method

Notes
