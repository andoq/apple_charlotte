Chicken in a Clay Pot

Ingredients

  • 6 dried black mushrooms
  • 4 chicken thighs, cut in half crosswise
  • Sauce:
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons Shao Hsing wine
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mashed fermented bean curd (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon white pepper

Method

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic -- minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger -- minced
  • 2 (about 2 oz each) Chinese sausages -- sliced
  • 3 green onions -- including tops, cut into 1 1/2" pcs.)
  • 1/4 cup bamboo shoots -- sliced
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 2 teaspoons water
  • green onions slivers for garnish

Notes

SERVINGS: 4

SOURCE: Martin Yan
