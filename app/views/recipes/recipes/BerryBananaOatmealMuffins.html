Berry Banana Oatmeal Muffins

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup AP flour
  • 3/4 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 cup oatmeal
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 mashed bananas
  • 1/2 cup soy milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups blueberries

Method

  • Mix together dry ingredients. Mix together wet ingredients and add to dry ingredients. Add vanilla and blueberries. Fill paper-lined muffin tins 3/4 full and bake at 400F for about 20 minutes.
