Carol Sanborn Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 9" pie crust
  • 9 apples, coursely grated
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1/2 cup oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar

Method

  • Preheat the oven to 325 degrees. Mix apples, sugar, cinnamon and flour together. Pour into prepared pie crust. Top apple mixture with nuts.
  • Crumble with fingers the remaining ingredients and place on top of nuts. Bake for about 45 minutes. Serve warm with ice cream.
