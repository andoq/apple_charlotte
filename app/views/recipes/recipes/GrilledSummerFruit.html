Grilled Summer Fruit

Ingredients

  • Selected summer or tropical fruit:
  • peaches, apricots, pineapple, mango,
  • bananas, etc., Sliced in large pieces.
  • Vegetable oil, brushed on lightly
  • Honey to taste

Method

  • Heat grill or grill pan until quite hot. Place fruit on grill and cook until caramelized and hot but not until mushy.
  • Place in service dish or bowl and top with ice cream or sorbet and fresh mint.

Notes

Description:

"This is an easy and fast dessert for a gang....Yes you can do it

ahead and serve it room temp (or reheat it quickly in the microwave)

but the fun is pulling it hot off the grill and serving with ice cream

or sorbet."
