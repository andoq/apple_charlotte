Jamaican "Jerk" Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, in pieces
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 6 tablespoons garlic, minced
  • 6 tablespoons onion, minced
  • 6 tablespoons dried minced onion
  • 2 tablespoons allspice
  • 1 tablespoon dried ground chipotle
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 4 1/2 teaspoons fresh thyme, minced
  • 4 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons nutmeg
  • 2 lemons, zest

Method

  • Mix all seasoning ingredients in a bowl. Place chicken in mixture and marinate for 4 hours or overnight. Bake in 375F oven for about 40 minutes and finish grilling on stovetop or outside grill just until done.

Notes

Description:

"Serve with a salsa, red chile sauce or fruit salsa to cool."

SERVINGS: 10
