Strawberry Crepes

Ingredients

  • Mix the following together in a blender
  • and let sit for 1/2 hour.
  • 1/2 cup AP flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup lukewarm water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
  • pinch of salt

Method

  • Place a small amount in crepe pan and cook until top is set.
  • Turn over with spatula or fingers.
  • Fill with fresh, sweetened strawberries and whipped cream.......fabulous for a hot summer evening.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8
