Orange Raisin Nut Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 whole orange, well washed
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 cup honey or white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup nuts

Method

  • Preheat oven to 400F.
  • Cut orange in pieces and remove pits. In food processor, put in first 4 ingredients. Blend for 3-4 minutes. Sift dry ingredients into a bowl. Mix in raisins & nuts. Blend again until mixed. Spoon into greased 9" cake pan. Bake at 350F for 25-30 minutes. ( If using honey, add with first 4 ingredients, if using sugar, add to dry mix).

Notes

SERVINGS: 12
