Chunky Baked Apples

Ingredients

  • 8-9 unpeeled apples, golden, fuji, gala, or mixture
  • 1-2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 tablespoons water, approximately

Method

  • Cut apples into large 1" chunks. Place in oven baking dish. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and pour water around bottom. Cover with aluminum foil. Bake in 375F oven for about 40-50 minutes until soft but not broken down.
  • Great with zabaione, ice cream or over a butter cake.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8

SOURCE: Apple Charlotte
