Chris's Pecan Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dark corn syrup
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar (+2 tbsp.??)
  • 1 cup pecans
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Method

  • Place in crust and bake about 40 minutes at 350.

Notes

NOTES:

NOTES : Chris claims this is the BEST pecan pie in the world!

SERVINGS: 8
