Chicken with Ginger (Gai phad king)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cloud ear mushrooms, soaked in water 10 minutes
  • 1/4 cup canola or soy oil
  • 14 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 halves chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups ginger, julienned
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1/3 cup oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soybean paste
  • 4 long red chiles, cut into 1" pieces
  • 3/4 cup chicken stock, or water
  • 16 green onions, chopped

Method

  • Take out hard core of the cloud ear mushrooms. Cut into pieces.
  • Heat oil in wok over high heat and stir fry garlic until just brown. Add onion and chicken stir-fry until chicken is opaque on all sides, approximately 3 minutes.
  • Add ginger and mushrooms, fish sauce, oyster sauce, soy sauce and soybean paste. Stir fry for about 2 minutes. Add chilies and stock, bring to a boil and cook 1 minute longer. Service with jasmine rice.

Notes

Description:

"One of our favorite easy, Thai dishes!"

SERVINGS: 8
