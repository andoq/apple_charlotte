You-Make-It Vegetable Soup

Ingredients

  • 3 quarts stock, chicken, vegetable or beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 leek, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • Some suggested soup items:
  • carrots, small dicde
  • celery, sliced thin
  • tomatoes, chopped
  • chicken or beef, cut or shredded
  • smoked sausage, sliced
  • cabbage, thinly sliced and chopped
  • zucchini, cubed or sliced
  • yellow summer squash, cubed or sliced
  • kidney beans, canned, rinsed, drained
  • garbanzo beans, canned, rinsed, drained
  • swiss chard, stems removed, chopped
  • potato, small dice
  • fresh herbs: basil, parsley, sage,
  • rosemary, etc.
  • pasta, cooked
  • cheese, shredded
  • Any fresh vegetables or ingredients that
  • are available are perfect for adding
  • to a soup.
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • juice of one lemon, or to taste

Method

  • Valentine Croutes:
  • 1 loaf artisan bread
  • olive oil
  • Place diced onion, leek and garlic in a bit of olive oil and cook until fragrant. Add stock. Kids can add as much or as little as they wish of the above ingredients. Cook for about 20-30 minutes until all the vegetables are cooked through. Wait until just before serving to add cooked pasta.
  • Top the served soup with a Valentine Croute and a bit of grated cheese.
  • Valentine Croutes: Slice the bread about 1/4" thin. With a heart-shaped cookie cutter, cut out of the center of each piece of bread a heart-shaped croute. Brush both sides with olive oil and grill on grill pan or saute pan until golden on both sides.

Notes

Description:

"This is a soup that kids can make all the choices about....using

whatever is around."

SERVINGS: 12
