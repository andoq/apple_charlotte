Sweet Pepper Salad

Ingredients

  • 8 bell pepper, multi-colored, roasted, skinned diced into 1" pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup Italian parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 preserved lemon, minced, or lemon juice and zest to taste

Method

  • Add peppers to pan with olive oil and seasonings. Cook until juices evaporate.

Notes

SERVINGS: 8

SOURCE: Adapted from Saveur, 1998.
