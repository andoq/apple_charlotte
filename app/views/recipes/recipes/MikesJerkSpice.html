Mike's Jerk Spice

Ingredients

  • 4 large onions, chopped
  • 6 cups brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground coriander
  • 4 tablespoons cumin
  • 2 tablespoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 4 tablespoons allspice
  • 4 tablespoons asil
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 6 tablespoons cayenne
  • 64 ounces canola oil
  • 4 tablespoons salt
  • 10 ounces fresh lime juice

Method

Notes

Combine all ingredients and mix well.SOURCE: Mike/Huckleberry's
