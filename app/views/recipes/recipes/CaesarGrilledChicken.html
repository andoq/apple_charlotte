CaesarGrilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 whole boneless chicken breast, cut in half (making 8 servings)
  • Apple Charlotte Caesar Dressing
  • 2 heads romaine lettuce
  • garlic croutons
  • Parmesan cheese

Method

  • Marinate the chicken breasts in the Caesar dressing for 15-20 minutes. Grill on hot grill for about 3-5 minutes. Turn over and turn heat down to medium and cover with lid. Continue to cook another 15 minutes or so until cooked through. Cool in frig.
  • Make a Caesar Salad by combining romaine and croutons with dressing and cheese. Serve with cold chicken sliced. Top with extra cheese. Yum!!

Notes

SERVINGS: 8
