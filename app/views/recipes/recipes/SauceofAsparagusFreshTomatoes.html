Sauce of Asparagus & Fresh Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. asparagus, sliced thin with large tips
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons onion, minced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded

Method

  • Saute onion, garlic until opague in olive oil. Add sliced asparagus without tips and cook slightly. Cut tomatoes in quarters. Add tips and tomatoes and cook briefly. Serve over pasta

Notes

Description:

"A light and bright addition to your summer menu items."

SERVINGS: 4

SOURCE: Fattoria Medicea, Pistoia
