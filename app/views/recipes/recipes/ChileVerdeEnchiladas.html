Chile Verde Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 1 recipe chile verde
  • 2 cups queso fresco, or Mexican cheese
  • corn or canola oil

Method

  • Place small amount of oil in sauce pan and warm. Briefly cook corn tortillas in oil to soften. Place a small amount chile verde and a bit of cheese in tortilla; fold over and place in oblong baking pan. Finish with all the tortillas and cover with a bit more sauce and cheese. Heat through until bubbly in 375F oven for about 20-30 minutes.

Notes

Description:

"These are made with one of my favorite Mexican sauces...Chile Verde."

SERVINGS: 6
