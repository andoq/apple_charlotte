Grilled Fresh Trout

Ingredients

  • 3 medium trout
  • olive oil spray
  • salt and pepper, or seasonings of choice
  • 3 large lemons, sliced thin

Method

  • Make tin foil "boats" and place a trout into each one. Spray inside of fish with olive oil spray and season with salt and pepper. Place sliced lemons inside the fish. Place in preheated outside grill, cover down and grill for 12-15 minutes.

Notes

Description:

"This is great fun for the "backyard campout" kids. They will love

it!"

SERVINGS: 8
