Croutons

Ingredients

  • 3 cups bread cubes
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled and smashed lightly
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cube butter
  • light sprinkling salt

Method

  • Place olive oil and butter in large hot saute pan. Add garlic and allow to infuse slightly at medium heat for about 3 minutes. Add bread cubes and coat with oil and butter. Begin to toast on top of stove and allow to cook for a long time (about 20 minutes) over lower heat until crispy, stirring every five minutes or so; OR place in 350F oven 10-13 minutes.

Notes

Description:

"Perfect for Ceasar's Salad and soups."
