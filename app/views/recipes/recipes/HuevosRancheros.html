Huevos Rancheros

Ingredients

  • tortillas, enough for 2 each guest
  • eggs, 2 for each guest
  • jack cheese or queso fresco
  • green onions or sliced white onion, sliced
  • chopped cilantro
  • Tomato Jalapeno Sauce

Method

  • Steam heat (in Chinese steamer over steaming water, wrapped in terry towel; one minute over heat; heat off for 10-15 minutes) or lightly saute in olive oil tortillas.
  • Poach or fry eggs.
  • Place two tortillas on plate slightly overlapping. Add two eggs on top. Spoon the tomato-jalapeno sauce over all. Sprinkle with onion, cheese, and cilantro. Enjoy!!
