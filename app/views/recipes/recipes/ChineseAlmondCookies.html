Chinese Almond Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup vegetable shortening
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 40 blanced almonds

Method

  • Sift flour, baking powder and baking soda into a bowl.
  • With an electric mixer, beat butter, shortening and sugars in a large bowl until fluffy. Add egg, vanilla and almond extract and; beat until blended. Add flour mixture; mix well. Shape dough into a ball and cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or up to 2 days.
  • Preheat oven to 350F. Divide dough into four pieces and each piece into 10 portions. Roll each portion into a ball, then place 2-3 inches apart on a baking sheet. Press a whole almond into the center of each ball.
  • Bake until golden brown, 14-16 minutes. Let cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a rack to cool completely. Store in an airtight container.
